My name is Pam. I am a lover of happiness AND our eco-system.

Like anyone else I am a consumer and love things to be convient. And that's okay! It's making the right choices that matters.

My green journey has been the most life changing experience that I wish I had done sooner. I love to help others blossom in their journey.

>Simply Green

Much like plants, you have to start from seed and then grow in to a beautiful healthy tree. In the begining, I had so many things that I threw away with little thought of what happens to them afterwards.

Making the switch was very simple. It took a bit of time and understanding, which really just enriches your mind. After you realize how you can still live a normal life and be eco-friendly it becomes second nature.

>Grow Together

I became enlighted and cured myself of this weight of selfishness. Since going green, the constant state of mindfulness has transformed me in to a more peaceful and happier person.
Inside and out. And you can too!
